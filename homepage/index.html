About me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Artificial Intelligence Lab of the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), under the supervision of Prof. Zhihai He. I am a visiting Ph.D. student of the Learning and Vision (LV) Lab at the National University of Singapore under the supervision of Prof. Xinchao Wang. I was an intern at the 2012 Laboratory of Huawei from 20221-2023.
I was a research intern at the 2012 Laboratory of Huawei from 2021-2023. Previously, I was an engineer at the TCL Huaxing Optoelectronic Semiconductor Display Technology Co. LTD from 2019-2020. I got Bachelor’s degree in 2019 at Harbin Engineering University (HEU).
My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, transfer learning, test-time adaptation, and Text-to-image generation.
